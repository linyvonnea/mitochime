\chapter{Supplementary Feature Importance Results Across Models}
\label{app:supp_feature_importance}

This appendix provides supplementary permutation-based feature importance results across several classical machine learning models. These results complement the main-text interpretation of the tuned Gradient Boosting classifier by showing how the most influential individual features and feature families compare across Gradient Boosting, CatBoost, LightGBM, Bagging Trees, and the multilayer perceptron (MLP).

\label{app:top10_importance_models}

\section{Aggregated Feature Family Importance by Model}
\label{app:family_importance_models}

Tables~\ref{tab:fam_gb}, \ref{tab:fam_catboost}, \ref{tab:fam_lightgbm}, \ref{tab:fam_bagging}, and \ref{tab:fam_mlp} summarize feature importance after aggregation by feature family. Across most models, the Clipping and Kmer\_jump families contribute the largest cumulative importance, while Micro\_homology contributes minimally. Gradient Boosting and Bagging Trees show slightly negative aggregate values for SA\_structure, indicating that this family contributed negligibly or inconsistently under permutation importance.
